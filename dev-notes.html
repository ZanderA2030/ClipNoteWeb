🪲 Bugs with Product Launch - 10/28/2025

It has come to our attention that there are a few bugs that have been identified with the initial launch of ClipNote. These bugs include:

The Ctrl + Enter shortcut not working for OperaGX;

The extension crashing after one use and not opening in OperaGX;

The summarize function not working correctly.

🚧 We are starting work on these issues ASAP and will have them resolved as soon as we can. 🚧
