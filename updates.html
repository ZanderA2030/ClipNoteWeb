Version 1.0.0 - 10/27/2025

ClipNote was launched today! This Google Chrome extension allows the user to seamlessly take notes online through the use of our quick and easy Ctrl + Enter shortcut, or through the classic copy-and-paste method! ClipNote also has a summarize function where the user can enter in paragraphs of information and ClipNote will summarize the information for you in clear, easy-to-read, information dense sentences! We hope you enjoy!
