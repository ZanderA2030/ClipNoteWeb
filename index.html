Capture and Cite Anything Instantly

Save, Organize, and Cite Anything You Read Online.

ClipNote lets you highlight text on any webpage and save it instantly with one shortcut — no sign-ups, no clutter, just clean, fast note-taking.

Features That Make ClipNote Stand Out

📌 Quick Save

Highlight text anywhere and press Ctrl + Enter (for Windows) or Cmd + Enter (for Mac) to save it instantly. No copying, no switching tabs.

🧠 Smart Cite

Quickly capture any website's URL to go along with your notes when the "Collect URL" box is checked.

🎨 Clean, Colorful Interface

A beautiful gradient design that's as simple as it is effective. No distractions, just notes.

🔒 100% Private

All your data stays in your browser - ClipNote never sends your information to any server. Your notes are stored locally and only you can see them.

✍ Easy to Manage

Copy or delete notes instantly with a single click. Keep everything tidy and accessible.

See it in Action

Highlight. Press. Saved. It's that easy.


Privacy You Can Trust

ClipNote never tracks you, never sends data to external servers, and never asks you for personal information. Everything happens right in your browser — fast, local, and secure.

Your data belongs to you. Always.

About ClipNote

ClipNote was made by me, Zander, to keep note-taking simple and productive. I wanted to build a faster way to capture ideas, quotes, and useful info from the web — without breaking focus. My goal is to keep ClipNote lightweight, private, and beautiful.

Privacy Policy

ClipNote accesses highlighted text on websites only to let you save it as a note. It does not collect, share, or transmit any data externally. All notes are stored securely in Chrome's local storage on your device.
